Frequently Asked Questions

How does StudySphere AI create flashcards?

+

StudySphere AI uses advanced natural language processing to analyze your study materials and automatically generate relevant flashcards. The AI identifies key concepts, definitions, and relationships in your text to create comprehensive study cards tailored to your content.

What file formats are supported?

+

We support multiple file formats including PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT files, and images (JPG, PNG, GIF). You can also paste text directly or record voice notes that our AI will transcribe and convert into flashcards.

How does spaced repetition work?

+

Spaced repetition is a scientifically proven learning technique that shows flashcards at increasing intervals based on your performance. Cards you know well appear less frequently, while difficult cards are shown more often to reinforce learning.

Is my data secure?

+

Yes, we take data security seriously. All your flashcards and personal information are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties, and you can delete your account and data at any time.

What's included in the Premium plan?

+

Premium includes unlimited flashcard creation, advanced analytics, priority support, export options, and access to premium AI features. You can compare plans on our pricing page.

Can I share flashcards with others?

+

Yes! You can share your flashcard sets with classmates, study groups, or make them public. Premium users also get collaboration features for group study sessions.
